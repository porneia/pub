Here's a simple and refreshing absinthe cocktail that's perfect for
spring.

The Artist's Garden:

1 part Pernod Absinthe
1/4 part diced cucumbers
2 parts ice cold club soda

Add ingredients to stemmed glass and garnish with cucumber wheel on rim.
